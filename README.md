# HR Analytics Dashboards in Excel: Workforce, Retention & Compensation Insights

## Overview  
This project presents a comprehensive HR analytics solution built in Excel to evaluate workforce performance, employee retention, and compensation efficiency.  

Three interactive dashboards were developed to address the needs of different stakeholders, enabling data-driven decision-making and improving visibility into key HR metrics.

---

## Purpose of the Project  
Organizations often struggle with limited visibility into workforce performance and employee-related costs. This project aims to bridge that gap by:

- Providing a centralized view of critical HR metrics  
- Identifying patterns in employee attrition and retention  
- Evaluating workforce productivity and cost efficiency  
- Supporting strategic planning through data-driven insights  

The dashboards are designed to simulate real-world business scenarios, where different teams require tailored insights to make informed decisions.

## Key Insights  

- Attrition is higher among specific age groups and job roles, indicating potential gaps in employee engagement and satisfaction.  
- The cost of employee turnover has a significant financial impact, emphasizing the importance of retention strategies.  
- Compensation growth does not always align with workforce productivity, highlighting opportunities for cost optimization.  
- Overtime and additional employee expenses contribute noticeably to overall operational costs.  
- Gender distribution and workforce demographics reveal areas for improving diversity and inclusion.
